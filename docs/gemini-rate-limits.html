Recommended Optimizations

4 Fixes (No Paid Plan Needed)

  • 1. Slim tool results — slice(0, 5) instead of (0, 30) → save ~30K tokens/brief
  • 2. Parallel tool calls — collapse 5 rounds into 2
  • 3. Cache briefs — reuse within a 10-minute window
  • 4. Manual role-switch brief — no auto-fire on viewer change
Combined effect: ~80% reduction in daily requests.
Free tier becomes comfortable for normal use.
